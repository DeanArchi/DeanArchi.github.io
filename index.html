Currency Convert Bot for Telegram

CurrencyConvertBot is a chatbot that helps you convert one currency to another conveniently and quickly at the current exchange rates. With this bot, you can easily compare exchange rates, get accurate conversions, and make money exchanges with minimal effort.

What this bot can do?

Currency conversion

Enter the amount and currency you need, and then specify the currency you want to convert to. The bot will provide you with the exact equivalent in the selected currency.

Latest exchange rates

Find out the latest exchange rates for different currencies. CurrencyConvertBot will provide you with updated information on exchange rates so that you can be confident in your financial calculations.

Exchange rate tracking

Add currency pairs to your watchlist to receive notifications when the exchange rate changes. You'll always be up-to-date with the latest changes and be able to make smart financial decisions.
